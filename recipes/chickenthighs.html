Chicken Thighs

Description

If you want a dinner that is simple, inexpensive, but full of flavor you don't need to look further than this recipe for crispy oven-roasted chicken thighs. Chicken thighs are dark meat which is the portion of the chicken that has more fat but that's where flavor comes from. We're leaving the skin on for even more flavor and of course the crispy skin. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Approximately 5 pounds of bone in and skin on chicken thighs
  • Salt
  • Garlic salt
  • Butter or non stick spray
  • Foil
  • Aluminum sheet pan-if you have a wire rack it's helpful but not necessary

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. While your oven is preheating line your pan with aluminum foil.
  3. Butter your foil prior to placing chicken thighs on it.
  4. Place the chicken thighs on the pan skin side up.
  5. Sprinkle salt and garlic salt on your thighs.
  6. Cook for approximately 35-40 minutes or until internal temperature is 165 degrees fahrenheit.
  7. Let rest for 5 minutes and then enjoy!
