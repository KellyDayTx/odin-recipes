Fried Eggs (over hard, break the yolks)

Description

Welcome to the wild world of fried eggs. If you've ever been to a Waffle House you have probably heard eggs ordered in a number of different ways. You may have your preferred order. Mine is "over hard break the yolks" when I order fried eggs. I do like scrambled but with restaurants using egg substitutes I like to know I'm getting real eggs.

Ingredients

  • Your preferred number and size of eggs (2 work for me as part of a breakfast)
  • Unsalted butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Non-stick pan
  • Spatula

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan to medium-high.
  2. Place a tablespoon of butter in the pan once it's up to temperature.
  3. Crack the egg (or eggs if the pan allows) into the melted butter.
  4. Use the egg shell to apply pressure to the yolk(s) until it breaks.
  5. Wait approximately 1 minute and then flip. If you're feeling fancy you can flip it like a pancake. This maneuver takes skill so be careful!
  6. Monitor the egg for your favorite doneness and then move them to a plate. Season to taste and enjoy!
